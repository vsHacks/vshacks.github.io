About Us

vsHacks vsHacks is a global virtual hackathon organized by students for students.

Established in 2022, vsHacks was originally organized by a group of several programming clubs across the Greater Vancouver Area.

With over 500 competitors in its history, it stands as one of the biggest virtual hackathons of its kind.
